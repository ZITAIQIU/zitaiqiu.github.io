I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Computing, Macquarie University, Australia. Supervised by A/Prof. Jia Wu . I am also a visiting scholar at the Max Planck Institute for Security and Privacy (MPI-SP) under the supervision of Prof. Gilles Barthe. I hold a Master's degree in Information and Technology from the University of Queensland, Australia. My research interests include social event detection, graph neural networks, hyperbolic space representation, heterogeneous information networks, and NLP.
